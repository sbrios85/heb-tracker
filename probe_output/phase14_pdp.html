Description

Ground from 100% Arabica beans, CAFE Olé by H-E-B Texas Pecan coffee is medium-bodied and brimming with the flavor of caramel and pecans. An aromatic treat as it brews, this medium roast blend is perfect for making a big pot or just a single cup with a reusable coffee pod filter.

• Medium Roast and medium bodied
• Texas Pecan: Creamy caramel and Texas pecan flavors

About CAFE Olé by H-E-B
Launched in 1997, CAFE Olé by H-E-B coffee is available in a variety of flavors and roasts. We begin with high quality Arabica beans harvested from legendary coffee-growing regions throughout the world. They are then roasted with precision and care to bring the artisan difference to your home. Enjoy exploring the complete collection of single cup, bagged ground, bulk beans and cold brew coffee.
